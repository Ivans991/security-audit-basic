# Nmap Scan Results

## Command Executed
nmap -sT -p 1-1024 127.0.0.1

## Purpose
This scan was performed to identify open TCP ports and listening services on the local system within a controlled and ethical scope.

## Summary of Results
- A limited number of common ports were identified as open.
- No unexpected or uncommon services were detected.
- Results are consistent with a typical workstation environment.

## Notes
This scan was executed only against the local system for educational purposes. No exploitation or intrusive actions were performed.
